# MTN-Customer-Churn-Analysis
The MTN Customer Churn Analysis is an end-to-end Business Intelligence solution developed using Microsoft Power BI to transform customer and revenue data into actionable business insights. The project focuses on understanding customer behavior, evaluating revenue performance, identifying the key drivers of customer churn, and supporting executive decision-making through interactive dashboards and data-driven recommendations.

# 🎯 Problem Statement
Customer retention is one of the most significant challenges facing the telecommunications industry. As competition intensifies, retaining existing customers becomes more cost-effective than acquiring new ones. High customer churn leads to revenue loss, increased acquisition costs, and reduced customer lifetime value, ultimately affecting long-term business performance.
MTN required a data-driven solution capable of consolidating customer, subscription, and revenue data into meaningful business intelligence.

# 📊 Project Objectives
The project aims to:
- Monitor customer churn and retention.
- Evaluate revenue performance.
- Analyze customer demographics.
- Measure customer lifetime value.
- Identify churn drivers.
- Support executive decision-making.
- Deliver actionable business recommendations.

# Executive Dashboard
Provides a high-level overview of business performance through KPIs such as Total Revenue, Total Customers, Churn Rate, Customer Lifetime Value, Revenue Lost to Churn, and Retention Rate. This dashboard enables executives to monitor organizational performance and make strategic decisions.

# Customer Analysis Dashboard
Analyzes customer demographics, geographic distribution, subscription plans, satisfaction levels, and tenure to understand customer behavior and identify high-value customer segments.

# Revenue Analysis Dashboard
Evaluates revenue trends across subscription plans, customer segments, purchase frequency, and geographic regions to identify growth opportunities and optimize revenue performance.

# Churn Deep Dive Dashboard
Investigates customer churn drivers by analyzing churn reasons, satisfaction ratings, subscription plans, tenure, and regional patterns to support targeted retention strategies.

# Customer Retention & Risk Dashboard
Identifies customers at risk of churning based on behavioral and satisfaction indicators, enabling proactive engagement and customer loyalty initiatives.

# 📈 Key Insights
The analysis revealed several critical insights:
- Approximately one-third of customers had churned, indicating a significant opportunity to strengthen customer retention strategies.
- Customers with lower satisfaction ratings exhibited a higher likelihood of churn.
- Certain subscription plans experienced higher churn rates than others.
- Broadband products generated the highest revenue and customer value.
- Customers with shorter tenures were more susceptible to churn.
- Geographic analysis identified regions requiring targeted customer retention initiatives.

# 💡 Strategic Recommendations
Based on the findings, the following strategic recommendations are proposed:
- Improve network quality and customer support to enhance customer satisfaction.
- Implement predictive churn analytics to identify and engage at-risk customers proactively.
- Optimize subscription plans through competitive pricing and flexible service packages.
- Introduce loyalty and rewards programs for long-term customers.
- Personalize customer engagement using demographic and behavioral segmentation.
- Continuously monitor customer satisfaction and resolve issues promptly.
- Leverage dashboard insights for ongoing strategic planning and performance management.
